About the Marin Headlands

The Marin Headlands is a hilly coastline in Marin Country, just across the Golden Gate Bridge from San Francisco, California.

It is part of the Golden Gate National Recreation Area, a protected area of the U.S. National Park Service, and is a popular site for hiking and recreation.

It also one of my personal favorite landscapes. I've visited this place countless times since childhood, and was compelled to write music inspired by the Headlands' atmosphere and to take many pictures and videos of it.
